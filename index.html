Shwetha Rajaram

I'm a PhD student at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I am a member of the Information Interaction Lab, advised by Dr. Michael Nebeling.

I study how we can design safer and more privacy-friendly augmented and virtual reality experiences. To do so, I develop novel interactive systems that allow me to investigate the implications of using AR/VR in various application areas and explore methods for integrating privacy guidelines in AR/VR practitioners' workflows.

Previously, I completed my undergrad in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Michigan.
