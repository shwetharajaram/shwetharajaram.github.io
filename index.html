Shwetha Rajaram

I'm a PhD student at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I am a member of the Information Interaction Lab, advised by Dr. Michael Nebeling.

I study how we can design more safe and privacy-friendly augmented and virtual reality systems. To accomplish this, I develop interactive systems to investigate the implications of using AR/VR in various application areas and explore methods for integrating privacy guidelines in AR/VR practitioners' workflows.

Previously, I completed my undergrad in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Michigan.
